hello, I'm Esther

I make silver jewellery by hand — each piece shaped, soldered, and finished in my studio.

I've always been drawn to the way metal holds marks: the texture of a hammer, the warmth of a flame, the small decisions that make each piece one-of-a-kind.

Everything is made to order, which means you'll never receive something mass-produced. When you wear a piece of mine, it's been made with your order in mind.
